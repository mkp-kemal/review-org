Frequently Asked Questions

Can we remove negative reviews?

No. To maintain trust and transparency, reviews are only removed if they violate our Community Guidelines (e.g., contain hate speech, threats, or private information). We encourage you to respond to negative reviews publicly and constructively with a Pro or Elite plan.

How do we claim our profile?

Search for your organization on our site. On your profile page, you will see a "Claim this Profile" button. You will be asked to verify your affiliation with the organization, typically through an official email address.

Are there discounts for paying annually?

Yes! We offer a 20% discount on all paid plans if you choose to pay annually. You can select this option during checkout.
